# Smooth Gamemode Switcher

Smooth Gamemode Switcher is a mod allowing you to have one hotkey to quickly switch between the gamemodes of your choice in your desired order. When switching from spectator to creative you will be in fly mode if you are at a specific height over the floor, you can adjust this number.
